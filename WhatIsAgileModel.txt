Agile software development methodology is 
an process for developing software (like 
other software development methodologies 
– Waterfall model, V-Model, Iterative model 
etc.) However, Agile methodology differs 
significantly from other methodologies. In English, 
Agile means ‘ability to move quickly and easily’ 
and responding swiftly to change – this is a key 
aspect of Agile software development as well.
